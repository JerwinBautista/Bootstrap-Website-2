About Haas F1 Team

Haas F1 Team is an American-licensed Formula One racing team established by NASCAR Cup Series team co-owner Gene Haas in April 2014. The team originally intended to make its debut at the start of the 2015 season but later elected to postpone their entry until the 2016 season. The team principal for the Haas F1 team is Guenther Steiner.

First F1 car

The VF-16 was the team's Debut car during the 2016 season. In the team's debut at the opening Australian Grand Prix, Grosjean finished 6th, scoring eight points for the team, which became the first American constructor to win points in its first F1 race and the first constructor overall since Toyota Racing in 2002 to record points in its debut.

Best season

The VF-18 was the best car and the 2018 season was also the team's best season to date, finishing fifth in the Constructors' Championship, one point short of doubling their previous year's performance.

2022 season

After a scoring drought in the 2021 season, Haas's decision to focus on building the VF-22 throughout the 2021 season resulted in the car proving to be competitive among the mid-field teams.
